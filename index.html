Who am i?

I'm Hanie Abbaspour, a multidisciplinary designer combining classical elegance with modern aesthetics. My focus is on branding, motion design, and expressive visuals inspired by Classic Art and contemporary storytelling.

Selected Works

HIRBODAN PAPER BAG "Nowruz Gift Bag"

This project aims to design a product that conveys a sense of authenticity, modernity, and Iranian culture at the same time using Iranian carpet motifs and warm traditional colors. This design was made as a special Nowruz giftbag by me for the Hirbodan management company.

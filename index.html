Who am I?

My designs

"Nowruz Gift Bag"

While designing this tote bag, I was thinking about how to blend Iranian traditions with modern design standards. The final result was a bag with a small front pocket inspired by the patterns of Persian carpets — a quiet nod to heritage. It was created as a New Year gift bag for clients and received an amazing response. What touched me most was that almost everyone, before even looking at the gift inside, said: “The bag is so beautiful!”

"Hirbodan Buisness Card"

The challenge? Hirbodan had always leaned into traditional elements and hadn’t had a fresh, impactful business card in years.

Now it was my turn to nudge the brand one step out of its old comfort zone.

I think the final result spoke for itself — and the satisfied smile on the department manager’s face said the rest.

Everyone who knows me knows how obsessed I am with planning. Without a plan, my whole life feels like it's spiraling out of control — and once I lose that sense of control, staying calm and focused becomes almost impossible. That’s why I always end up creating my own custom planners by hand. I’ve never found a planner out there that truly checks all my boxes. So, with all that said, you can probably imagine how excited I was when my manager said we needed to design a planner for our clients. I was like: "Trust me on this — just hand it over to me!" And guess what? My manager loved the result so much that there wasn’t even a single comment or edit. She immediately said: “It’s perfect! Let’s print it — how soon can we have it ready?”

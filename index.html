Who am I?

I'm Hanie Abbaspour, a multidisciplinary designer combining classical elegance with modern aesthetics. My focus is on branding, motion design, and expressive visuals inspired by Classic Art and contemporary storytelling.

2019

Started my career in graphic design, focusing on branding and visual identity. Worked with small clients to build my foundational skills.

2020

Joined a digital marketing agency as a motion designer. Created animated content for social campaigns and learned video production tools.

2021

Launched my own personal brand and began taking freelance clients. Developed a clean visual style and showcased my work online.
